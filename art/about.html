Sarah Ransohoff is a graduate of the University of North Carolina (Bachelor of Arts degree), and lives and works as a programmer and illustrator in Brooklyn. She draws editorial illustrations (clients include The New Yorker), cartoons, and paints in watercolor, ink, and oils. Sarah also does improv comedy in NYC each week at Magnet Theater. Her combined love of drawing and comedy will be coming together this June in the show You Don't Have To Laugh, where comedians showcase their artwork and perform improv.
