Sarah Ransohoff is a graduate of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and currently resides in Brooklyn working as a programmer and illustrator. She draws editorial illustrations (clients include The New Yorker), cartoons, and paints in watercolor, ink, acrylic and oils. For six years she studied and performed improv comedy in Chapel Hill and NYC, including a year run when she performed weekly on a house team at Magnet Theater.
